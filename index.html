How It Works

A short explanation you can use directly while using the fuzzy logic smart fan controller.

  1. Fuzzification converts crisp inputs into fuzzy membership degrees.
  2. Rules compare the input states, such as IF temperature is hot AND humidity is humid THEN fan speed is high.
  3. Each rule fires with a strength based on the minimum of its input memberships.
  4. The output fuzzy sets are combined and converted into one crisp value using the centroid method.

What These Terms Mean in Values

The system uses overlapping fuzzy ranges, so one input can belong partly to two labels at the same time. These values help users understand what the words on the screen mean while adjusting the sliders.

Temperature

  • Cold: fully cold at 12C and below, fades until 22C
  • Pleasant: active from 18C to 32C, peak at 25C
  • Hot: starts from 28C, fully hot at 38C and above

Humidity

  • Dry: fully dry at 30% and below, fades until 50%
  • Normal: active from 40% to 70%, peak at 55%
  • Humid: starts from 60%, fully humid at 80% and above

Fan Speed Percentage

  • Low: fully low at 25% and below, fades until 45%
  • Medium: active from 35% to 75%, peak at 55%
  • High: starts from 65%, fully high at 85% and above
